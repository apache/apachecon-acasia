RustFS Scaling Laws: Performance Analysis and Insights from a more 200-Node Distributed Cluster

Jinghe Ma

Chinese Session   #rust

As a distributed object storage solution, RustFS relies on horizontal scalability to drive capacity growth. To guarantee a robust and dependable scaling mechanism, our team executed large-scale testing involving hundreds of nodes. This presentation deep dives into the performance benchmarking and analytical methodologies used during our 200+ node RustFS cluster expansion.

Speakers:


Jinghe Ma: RustFS, Core contributior

  1. The core contributor for RustFS, focusing on Docker/Helm chart development as well as issue address.
  2. Was a DevOps/DevSecOps engineer. Focusing on DevOps/DevSecOps/ChatOps/open-source software supply security. Shared best practices on SECon, TiD, DevOps community summit, etc.
  3. The member of the Linux Foundation Evangelist and the member of the China Cloud Native community and China DevOps community.
