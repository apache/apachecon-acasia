This session begins with an introduction to the groundbreaking feature of Apache SkyWalking 10.4: AI Observability. We will explore the strategic importance of monitoring Large Language Model (LLM) applications and the role of Envoy AI Gateway in securing and managing AI traffic at the infrastructure edge.

The core of the talk will focus on the technical architecture and principles of this new feature. Using the SkyWalking Spring AI agent plugin and Envoy AI Gateway as primary examples, we will demonstrate how telemetry data is captured across different layers of the AI stack. We will then dive into how the Observability Analysis Platform (OAP) processes this data, specifically focusing on the transformation of OTLP and Zipkin formatted traces into actionable statistical metrics. The workflow concludes with a showcase of how these insights are visualized through out-of-the-box dashboards.

To provide a broader perspective, we will conclude by discussing the current AI Observability landscape. We will examine industry leaders such as Langfuse and Arize AI, analyzing their unique approaches—ranging from LLM lifecycle tracing and prompt management to model evaluation and guardrails—and how these compare with SkyWalking’s ecosystem.
