DORA: An embodied intelligent robot development system built using the Rust programming language. This section describes DORA's architecture, operating mechanisms, and performance advantages, highlighting its many features.

Leon Li: DORA Community Technical Lead

The DORA community's technical lead is responsible for the entire DORA project's R&D process and ecosystem construction and implementation. He has extensive practical experience in robot application R&D and end-to-end solution delivery, and currently focuses on the requirements breakdown, architecture design, and technology selection of embodied intelligent robot systems.
