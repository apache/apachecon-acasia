Modern organizations are increasingly seeking a unified platform that combines data integration, governance, lineage, and operational applications—similar to what platforms like Palantir Foundry provide. However, building such a system from scratch often appears prohibitively complex.

This session presents a pragmatic, engineering-driven approach to constructing a “Foundry-like” platform using the Apache ecosystem.
