As Time-Series Foundation Models (TSFMs) like Timer rapidly scale up, a critical gap has emerged between algorithmic capabilities and underlying system infrastructure. In massive Industrial IoT scenarios, traditional data pipelines (e.g., exporting databases to CSV/Numpy) introduce severe I/O bottlenecks. Furthermore, fine-grained MoE time-series architectures face immense memory and communication walls during training.
This session introduces a high-performance, end-to-end AI training infrastructure built natively on Apache TsFile.
