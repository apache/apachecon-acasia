2026 is the year AI agents went from demos to production. Every agent — whether querying data, training models, or orchestrating pipelines — hits the same wall: "Where is the data, and do I have permission?" The metadata catalog is no longer a passive registry; it is the contextual brain agents depend on to discover, reason, and act.

This talk shares how we built TCCatalog on Apache Gravitino to serve this new role at Tencent Cloud scale. We cover cloud-native architecture, extensions for AI-native workloads (Iceberg + Lance + Model Registry + MCP), and hard-won production lessons — including real incident stories, performance data, and engineering trade-offs.
